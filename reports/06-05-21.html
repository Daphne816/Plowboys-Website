Game Report

06/05/2021
Cantigny Park Event

The Plowboys competed at Cantigny Park in Wheaton, IL on Saturday and scored 3 victories against teams from Illinois and Wisconsin.

Game 1 pitted the Plowboys against the Oak Leafs from Waukesha, Wi. This game was all Plowboys as they tallied 4 aces in the second inning and never looked back, ultimately winning 5 to 1. Aces were tallied by David "Eyes" Selke, Steve "Ironhead" South, Ric "Lucky" Hanrahan and 2 by Daniel "Clutch" Lech. Aces batted in went to "Lucky" in the third inning and Alan "Cracker Jack" Baldwin in the second.

Game 2 had the Plowboys take on the Oregon Ganymedes. If the last game was all Plowboys, this game was ALL Plowboys as they laid out a 9 ace second inning led by a 3 ace four bagger by Sherman "Snake Oil" Smith. All of the Plowboys had a hit and all tallied at least 1 ace! The Ganymedes put on a late charge but were shut down as the Plowboys went on to win 13 to 6.

Game 3 set the Plowboys against the Beloit Olympians. The Olympians were more than worthy opponents and showed early that they were here to win. They took off with a 6 ace first inning and an 8 to 4 lead after the second. The Plowboys' strikers came to life in the third inning and tallied 6 aces led by a grand slam four bagger by Antonio "Jackrabbit" Bellino. The Plowboys put another 4 aces on the board in the fifth inning and rallied to take the win 14 to 13.
